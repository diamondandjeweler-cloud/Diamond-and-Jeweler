**To:** [counsel email]
**Subject:** Pre-launch legal review — 3 questions gating our pilot (recruitment platform, PDPA + discrimination)

Dear [Counsel name],

We are preparing to launch **BoLe** (diamondandjeweler.com), a Malaysia-first, two-sided AI-curated
recruitment platform. We are **pre-launch with zero real users**, and before we open to our first
signups we want your read on **three items** — the attached packet sets out each one with our lay
assumptions for you to confirm or correct.

**The one we most need your view on (Item 1):** our matching score is influenced by a candidate's
**date of birth** (an internal "compatibility" signal), and onboarding collects **race** and
**religion**. Because a low internal score can reduce whether a candidate is surfaced to an employer,
we are concerned about two things under Malaysian law:

1. **PDPA 2010 (as amended 2024):** religion is sensitive personal data; race and DOB-derived
   inferences may attract heightened obligations. Is collecting/processing these for a
   matching/ranking purpose lawful **with explicit consent**, or impermissible regardless of consent?
2. **Discrimination exposure:** does using these attributes to influence candidate *visibility to
   employers* create discrimination risk for us and/or our hiring customers?
   If risky, is the safer path to (i) drop race/religion, (ii) stop using DOB as a ranking input, or
   (iii) keep them behind a specific consent + disclosure regime you draft?

**Items 2 and 3 are small and well-scoped:**
- **Item 2:** our hiring-side consent contains a "Waiver of Claims" of PDPA rights that we believe is
  unenforceable and contradicts our own Privacy Notice. May we **delete it and rely on our ToS §8
  liability cap**, or would you give replacement wording?
- **Item 3:** our Terms lack a **user-content licence** to host/store/display uploaded résumés and
  profile data (the platform's core function). We propose a narrow, non-sublicensing licence limited
  to operating the service — we'd like your approved wording.

We can remove or gate any of the Item-1 attributes in the product **within hours** of your advice, so
your turnaround directly sets our pilot date. A short memo or inline redlines on the three points is
all we need.

Thank you very much.

[Owner name]
BoLe / DNJ — diamondandjeweler.com
